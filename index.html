About the University

JK Lakshmipat University (jklu) was established in jaipur, the capital city of rajasthan, in the year 2011 to give students the cutting-edge of holistic learning and to develop promising youngsters into leaders of tomorrow with globally relevant, future-ready and actionable intelligence.The University has excellent infrastructure, a proper mix of experienced, young and dynamic faculty and a strong focus on placements for building technocrats and leaders of tomorrow. The curriculum provides for high weightage on internship to develop employable professionals with the cooperation of business and industry.
